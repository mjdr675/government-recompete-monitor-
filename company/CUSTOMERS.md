# CUSTOMERS.md — Customer Registry

This file tracks every customer conversation, trial, and paying account.
It is the sales CRM until we need a real one.

---

## How to Use This File

When you talk to a potential customer:
1. Add a row to the Pipeline table
2. Note what they told you about their problems (direct quotes are gold)
3. Update status as the conversation progresses
4. Move to Customers table when they pay
