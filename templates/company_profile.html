Contract Import Details

Recompete uses these details to find contracts your business already holds in federal award data. Providing your UEI or exact vendor name enables automatic matching on the dashboard.

Find or request your UEI at SAM.gov
5-character code from SAM.gov
The exact vendor name on your USASpending or SAM.gov contracts. Used as a fallback when UEI is not available.
Why we ask for this
Your UEI (Unique Entity Identifier) is the primary identifier the federal government uses to track contract awards in SAM.gov. When you provide it, Recompete can precisely match your existing contracts in USASpending data. CAGE Code is an alternative identifier used by DoD and some civilian agencies. If neither is available, we fall back to matching your vendor name across award records — slightly less precise but still effective for most businesses.
