How is the Recompete Score calculated?

The score (0–100) ranks how attractive a contract is to pursue as a recompete. It adds up:

  • Competition type — up to 40 pts (full & open competition scores highest)
  • Contract value — up to 35 pts (larger awards score higher)
  • Time remaining — up to 25 pts (sooner expirations score higher)
  • Agency & sourcing signals — small bonuses (up to 15 pts) for priority agencies, a known solicitation, and key contracting offices

Priority tiers: CRITICAL 90+, HIGH 75–89, MEDIUM 60–74, LOW below 60.
