Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026 · Effective date: July 5, 2026

Plain-English summary. We collect the data you give us (email, company, plan) and a small amount we observe (device, interactions). We use it to run the product, keep it safe, and bill you. We do not sell your data. We use Stripe for payments and a few named vendors for infrastructure. You can email us to see, correct, or delete your data.

1. Who this policy applies to

This Privacy Policy describes how Recompete LLC (“Recompete,” “we,” “us”) collects and uses personal information when you visit recompete.us or use the Service. It applies to visitors, trial users, and paying customers located in the United States and its territories. The Service is not directed at residents of the European Union or the United Kingdom.

2. What we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account: email, password (hashed), company name, plan.
  • Billing: name, billing address, and payment method — collected and stored by our payment processor, Stripe. We receive only a redacted card summary and a customer/subscription reference.
  • Support: content of emails or chats you send us.
  • Optional profile: agencies, NAICS/PSC codes, and geographic preferences you save.

2.2 Information we observe

  • Device and browser type, IP address, approximate location (city/region derived from IP).
  • Product-usage events: pages viewed, features used, watchlist and search activity.
  • Cookies and similar technologies as described in Section 7.

2.3 Public government data

The Service surfaces public U.S. federal procurement records (e.g., SAM.gov, USAspending). This data is public and is not your personal information; it is part of the product.

3. How we use it

  • Provide, secure, and improve the Service.
  • Bill you and process refunds.
  • Send transactional messages (receipts, password resets, alerts you subscribed to).
  • Detect abuse and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Send occasional product updates. You can opt out at any time from the email footer.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with vendors that help us operate:

  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • Railway — application hosting and database.
  • Resend — transactional and notification email delivery.
  • HubSpot — customer relationship management for account outreach and lifecycle communication.
  • Sentry — application error monitoring.

Each vendor is bound by a data processing agreement and processes data only on our instructions. We also share information if required by law, to enforce our rights, or in connection with a merger or acquisition.

5. Data retention

We keep account and billing records for the life of your account and for up to seven (7) years after account closure to satisfy tax and audit requirements. Product-usage logs are retained for 12 months. You can request earlier deletion under Section 8.

6. Cookies

We use a small number of first-party cookies for authentication, preference storage (e.g., theme), and CSRF protection. We do not currently set third-party analytics or advertising cookies.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures — encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+), encrypted database backups, least-privilege access for staff, and audit logging on billing writes. No system is perfectly secure; if you spot a vulnerability, please report it to security@recompete.us.

8. Your rights

You can request to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information by emailing privacy@recompete.us. We will respond within 30 days.

California residents: under the CCPA/CPRA, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to correct or delete it, and to opt out of “sale” or “sharing” (we do neither). To exercise these rights, email privacy@recompete.us.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has given us data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Where we operate

Recompete operates from the United States and processes your data here. The Service is not offered to residents of the European Union or the United Kingdom.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date and, where required, notified by email.

12. Contact

Questions or requests? Email privacy@recompete.us. Email that address to request our current mailing address if you need one for written correspondence.
