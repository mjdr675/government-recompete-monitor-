Refund Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026 · Effective date: July 5, 2026

Plain-English summary. Cancel any time, keep access until your period ends, and we do not automatically prorate. If you cancel within 14 days of your first paid charge and have not heavily used the product, email us and we will refund it. After that we handle refunds case by case.

1. Cancellation

You can cancel your Recompete subscription at any time from the billing settings page or by opening the Stripe billing portal from that page. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period; you will not be charged again after that.

You keep access to the Service for the remainder of the paid period. We do not automatically prorate partial periods.

2. Free trial

Where offered, our free trial gives you full access for seven (7) days on annual Payment Links without charge. Cancel before the trial ends to avoid being billed. If we bill you because you did not cancel in time, see Section 3 below.

3. 14-day refund window (first paid charge)

Recompete offers a good-faith refund on your first paid subscription charge if all of the following apply:

  • You request the refund within 14 days of the charge.
  • Your account has not been used to export more than 500 contract records or to materially process a full watchlist cycle.
  • The account is in good standing under our Terms of Service.

To request a refund, email billing@recompete.us from the address on your account and reference your workspace name. We aim to respond within two (2) business days.

4. Renewals and yearly plans

Yearly subscriptions renew automatically at the end of the term at the then-current rate. To avoid renewal, cancel before your renewal date. Once a yearly renewal has been charged, we offer a 7-day refund window on that renewal charge if you email billing@recompete.us before the 7-day window closes.

5. Failed payments

If a scheduled payment fails, Stripe will retry per its dunning schedule. If retries fail, we will suspend paid features and notify you by email. You can update your payment method any time from the billing portal. Suspended accounts are not eligible for refunds for the suspended period.

6. Chargebacks

Please contact us before initiating a chargeback with your card issuer. Most billing disputes can be resolved directly and faster than a chargeback. We reserve the right to suspend accounts with unresolved chargebacks pending investigation.

7. Exceptional circumstances

If you experienced a material Service outage or a bug that prevented you from using the product for an extended period, email us — we handle those cases individually and may offer a partial refund or a service credit even outside the 14-day window.

8. Contact

Billing questions? billing@recompete.us. We respond within one (1) business day.
