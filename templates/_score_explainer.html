The Recompete Score (0–100) ranks how attractive a contract is to pursue as a recompete opportunity. A higher score means you should act sooner. It adds up four components:

  • Competition type — up to 40 pts. Full & open competition scores highest because the field is widest and no incumbent advantage exists.
  • Contract value — up to 35 pts. Larger awards take more effort but offer greater revenue; the score rewards contracts over $1M.
  • Time remaining — up to 25 pts. Contracts expiring sooner score higher because the opportunity window is closing.
  • Agency & sourcing signals — up to 15 bonus pts for priority agencies, a known solicitation number, and key contracting offices.

Priority tiers: CRITICAL 90+   HIGH 75–89   MEDIUM 60–74   LOW below 60

Tip: Use the Views page to browse contracts filtered to CRITICAL or expiring soon.
